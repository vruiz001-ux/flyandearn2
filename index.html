Simple & Legal Process

How FlyAndEarn Works

A service fee model that stays 100% within EU duty-free allowances. No commercial import — just travelers helping buyers.

🛒 Buyer — Save 20–40% on duty-free goods

1

Create a Request

Tell us what you want: duty-free spirits, perfume, electronics — OR any item from a store abroad.

2

Get Matched

We connect you with verified travelers heading your way.

3

Receive & Save

Meet your traveler, verify the product. Save 20-40% on duty-free, or get items you can't find locally.

🧳 Traveler — Earn a service fee on every trip

1

Post Your Trip

Share your upcoming flight details — departure, destination, and travel dates.

2

Accept Requests

Browse duty-free AND retail shopping requests along your route.

3

Buy, Deliver & Earn

Shop at duty-free or any local store, meet the buyer, earn your fee (15-20%).

💡 Two Ways to Use FlyAndEarn:

🛃 Duty-Free Service: Travelers use their personal EU customs allowance to buy duty-free goods for buyers. The buyer pays the duty-free price + a 15% service fee. 100% legal — no commercial import.

🛍️ Shop Anywhere Service: Travelers buy items from any retail store at their destination. Perfect for getting brands, products, or specialties unavailable in the buyer's country. 20% service fee. Note: standard customs duties may apply to imported retail goods.
