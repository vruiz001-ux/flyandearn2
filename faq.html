Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FlyAndEarn. For more details about costs, check our pricing plans.

General

What is FlyAndEarn?
FlyAndEarn is a peer-to-peer marketplace that connects travelers with shoppers who want to purchase duty-free items. Travelers can earn money by bringing back duty-free products for others, while shoppers get access to duty-free prices without traveling.
How does it work?
For Shoppers: Create a request specifying what you want, your budget, and where you need it delivered. Travelers heading to relevant destinations will see your request and can offer to fulfill it.

For Travelers: Browse requests that match your upcoming trips, make offers, and earn money by purchasing and delivering duty-free items on your way.
Is FlyAndEarn legal?
Yes! FlyAndEarn facilitates peer-to-peer shopping transactions. However, both shoppers and travelers must comply with customs regulations and duty-free allowances in their respective countries. Each person is responsible for understanding and following local laws.

Payments & Security

How are payments handled?
We use Stripe for secure payment processing. When a shopper pays for a request, funds are held in escrow until the delivery is confirmed. This protects both parties in the transaction.
When does the traveler get paid?
Funds are released to the traveler's wallet when the shopper confirms receipt of the items, or automatically after 14 days if no dispute is raised. Travelers can then withdraw their earnings to their bank account.
What if there's a problem with my order?
If items are damaged, incorrect, or not delivered, you can open a dispute within 14 days of the expected delivery date. Our support team will investigate and may issue a partial or full refund depending on the situation.
What fees does FlyAndEarn charge?
Shoppers: A small service fee is added to each transaction to cover payment processing and platform costs.

Travelers: A small platform fee is deducted from earnings. Premium subscription members enjoy reduced fees.

For Shoppers

How do I create a request?
Click "Create Request" on the homepage or dashboard. Specify the product you want, your budget, delivery location, and any preferences. Travelers will then be able to see your request and make offers.
Can I request any product?
You can request most duty-free items including perfumes, cosmetics, alcohol, tobacco, electronics, and luxury goods. However, prohibited items (weapons, illegal substances, etc.) are strictly forbidden. All products must comply with customs regulations.
How do I know I can trust a traveler?
All travelers have public profiles showing their ratings, completed transactions, and verification status. We recommend choosing travelers with positive reviews. Plus, our escrow system protects your payment until you confirm delivery.

For Travelers

How do I start earning?
1. Create an account and verify your email
2. Add your upcoming trip details
3. Browse requests that match your route
4. Make offers on requests you can fulfill
5. Once accepted, purchase the items and deliver them
6. Get paid when delivery is confirmed!
How much can I earn?
Earnings vary based on the products you deliver and your service fee. Many travelers earn between 10-20% of the product value as their fee. High-demand items and premium service can command higher rates. Check our pricing page for detailed fee structure information.
What about customs limits?
Each country has duty-free allowances. As a traveler, you're responsible for staying within these limits. We recommend familiarizing yourself with the customs regulations of both your departure and arrival countries.
