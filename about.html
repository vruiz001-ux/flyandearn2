About FlyAndEarn

Connecting travelers and buyers to unlock savings through duty-free allowances and global shopping.

Company Information

Legal Entity: Tropos Sp. z o.o.

Registration: Polish registered company

Location: Warsaw, Poland

Our Mission

At FlyAndEarn, we're on a mission to connect travelers and buyers to unlock savings through duty-free allowances and global shopping. We believe that everyone should have access to great products at fair prices, regardless of where they are in the world.

Our platform facilitates two types of connections: travelers can help others by purchasing duty-free goods during their journeys, while buyers can access products from anywhere in the world through our network of helpful travelers.

How It Started

FlyAndEarn was founded by aviation enthusiasts who noticed a common problem: travelers were leaving their valuable duty-free allowances unused. Every day, millions of passengers pass through airports with the legal right to purchase duty-free goods, but many don't take advantage of this opportunity.

At the same time, we saw people struggling to access specific products that weren't available in their region or were significantly more expensive locally. The solution was clear: create a platform that connects these two groups in a way that benefits everyone.
