Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try FlyAndEarn for free?
Yes! Our Free tier allows you to complete 2 transactions per year without any subscription. It's perfect for trying out the platform and seeing how much you can save.
What counts as a transaction?
One transaction equals one completed order, regardless of the number of items in that order. For example, if you order 3 different products in a single request, that counts as 1 transaction.
Can I switch plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll pay the prorated difference. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes, we offer pro-rated refunds within 30 days of purchase. If you're not satisfied with your subscription, contact us and we'll refund the unused portion of your plan.
