5. Service Fee Model

5.1 How It Works

FlyAndEarn operates on a service fee model:

  1. Buyers post requests for duty-free goods
  2. Travelers offer to fulfill requests for a service fee
  3. Buyers pay for goods + service fee + platform fee
  4. Funds are held in escrow until delivery confirmation
  5. Travelers receive payment after successful delivery

5.2 Fees

Fee Type Amount Paid By
Service Fee (to Traveler) Set by Buyer (max 15% of product price) Buyer
Platform Fee 10% of total + €0.50 Buyer

5.3 Escrow

All payments are held in escrow until:

  • The Buyer confirms delivery, OR
  • 14 days pass without a dispute, OR
  • A dispute is resolved

6. EU Customs Compliance

Personal Import Allowances (Non-EU to EU)

Travelers arriving from outside the EU by air may bring:

  • Alcohol: 1L spirits (>22% ABV) OR 2L wines/spirits (≤22% ABV), plus 4L wine, plus 16L beer
  • Tobacco: 200 cigarettes OR 50 cigars OR 250g tobacco
  • Other goods: Up to €430 in value

These allowances are for personal use only. Exceeding these limits or commercial activity may result in customs duties, fines, or legal action.
